Overview

This is a data service that facilitates access to a variety of Alaska and Arctic geospatial data.

The code is hosted on Github. Instructions on how to use each data endpoint is given below.

Service endpoints

  • ALFRESCO model outputs
  • Spruce Beetle Risk
  • Design Index
  • Digital Elevation Models (DEMs)
  • Forests and Vegetation
  • Geology
  • Glaciology
  • Landfast Sea Ice
  • Mean Annual Precipitation
  • Permafrost
  • Physical and Administrative Boundary Polygons
  • Physiography
  • Sea Ice Concentration
  • Temperature and Precipitation
  • Temperature, Precipitation, Snow, Degree Days and Sea Ice Min, Mean, and Max
  • Wildfire
