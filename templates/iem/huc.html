IEM HUC Aggregation

Query SNAP's 771m downscaled AR5/CMIP5 temperature and precipitation data for a specific HUC and aggregate by mean.

Example: /iem/huc/19070506

CSV Export

To export the aggregated HUC data in a CSV file, append ?format=csv.

Example: /iem/huc/19070506?format=csv

8-digit HUC shapefiles accessed at https://datagateway.nrcs.usda.gov/
