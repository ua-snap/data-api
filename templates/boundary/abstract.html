Boundary data

These data are intended for exposing the polygon boundaries used across SNAP tools and data. Each query for a polygon will return the representative GeoJSON.

Service Endpoints

  • Area of Interest Query: All Polygon Boundaries
  • Climate Divisions: Alaska Climate Divisions
  • Corporations: Alaska Native Corporations
  • Ethnolinguistic Regions: Ethnolinguistic Regions of Alaska and parts of Canada.
  • Fire Management: Wildfire Management Zones for Alaska
  • Hydrologic Units (HUCs): HUCs of Alaska
  • Protected Area: Protected areas (parks, refuges, reserves, etc.) of Alaska, Yukon, and British Columbia.
