Boundary Data

These endpoints provide access to a common set of areas, and their respective boundary data, used across SNAP tools and data.

Service endpoints

Available places

Query a list of community or areas of interest by type.

Endpoint Example URL
All available areas of interest /places/all
Communities /places/communities
Hydrological unit codes (HUCs) /places/hucs
Alaska, British Columbia, or Yukon protected areas /places/protected_areas
Alaska Native corporations /places/corporations
Alaska climate divisions /places/climate_divisions
Fire management zones /places/fire_zones
Alaska ethnolinguistic regions /places/ethnolinguistic_regions
Alaska boroughs (county equivalent) /places/boroughs
Alaska census areas for the Unorganized Borough /places/census_areas
Game management units /places/game_management_units
First nations /places/first_nations

Yukon game management subzones /places/yt_game_management_subzones

CSV output is also available by appending ?format=csv to the URL.
