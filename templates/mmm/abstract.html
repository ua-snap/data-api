Historical and Projected Minimum, Mean, and Maximum

The endpoint shere provide access to the temperature at surface in degrees Celsius, total annual precipitation in mm, and degree days for both historical and projected model data over the state of Alaska.

Service endpoints

These endpoints are for point queries to generate TAS, PR, and degree days min, mean, and max for a given point.

  • Temperature Point Query: TAS min, mean, and max for point.
  • Precipitation Point Query: PR min, mean, and max for point.
  • Degree Days Point Query: Min, mean, and max at point for various degree day thresholds.
