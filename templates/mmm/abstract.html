Historical and Projected Temperature + Precipitation Minimum, Mean, and Maximum

The endpoint here provides access to the temperature at surface in degrees Fahrenheit and total annual precipitation in inches for both historical and projected model data over the state of Alaska.

Service endpoints

These endpoints are for point queries to generate temperature and precipitation minimum, mean, and maximum for a given point.

  • Temperature Point Query: min, mean, and max temperature values for point.
  • Precipitation Point Query: min, mean, and max precipitation values for point.
