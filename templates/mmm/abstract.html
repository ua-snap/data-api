Historical and Projected Temperature + Precipitation Minimum, Mean, and Maximum

The endpoint here provides access to the temperature at surface in degrees Celsius and total annual precipitation in mm for both historical and projected model data over the state of Alaska.

Service endpoints

These endpoints are for point queries to generate TAS and PR min, mean, and max for a given point.

  • Temperature Point Query: TAS min, mean, and max for point.
  • Precipitation Point Query: PR min, mean, and max for point.
