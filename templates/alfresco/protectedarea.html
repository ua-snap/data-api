ALFRESCO Protected Area Aggregation

Query data derived from 1km ALFRESCO outputs for a specific protected area and aggregate by mean.

Examples for the currently available datasets:

  • Example: /alfresco/flammability/protectedarea/NPS7
  • Example: /alfresco/veg_change/protectedarea/NPS7

CSV Export

To export the aggregated protected area data in a CSV file, append ?format=csv.

Example: /alfresco/flammability/protectedarea/NPS7?format=csv
