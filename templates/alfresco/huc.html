ALFRESCO HUC aggregation

Query data derived from 1km ALFRESCO outputs for a specific HUC and aggregate by mean.

Examples for the currently available datasets:

  • Relative flammability: /alfresco/flammability/huc/19080309
  • Relative vegetation change: /alfresco/veg_change/huc/19080309

CSV Export

To export the aggregated HUC data in a CSV file, append ?format=csv.

Example: /alfresco/flammability/huc/19080309?format=csv

8-digit HUC shapefiles accessed at https://datagateway.nrcs.usda.gov/
