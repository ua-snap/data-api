Temperature and Precipitation Protected Area Aggregation

Query SNAP's 2km downscaled temperature or precipitation data for a specific protected area and aggregate by mean.

Example: /temperature/protectedarea/NPS7

Example: /precipitation/protectedarea/NPS7

Query both temperature and precipitation data for a single protected area: /taspr/protectedarea/NPS7

CSV Export

To export the aggregated protected area data in a CSV file, append ?format=csv.

Example: /temperature/protectedarea/NPS7?format=csv

Example: /taspr/protectedarea/NPS7?format=csv
