Temperature and Precipitation Geospatial Polygon Aggregation

Query SNAP's 2km downscaled temperature or precipitation data for a specific area of interest polygon ID and aggregate by mean.

Example: /temperature/area/19010208

Example: /precipitation/area/19010208

Query both temperature and precipitation data for an example HUC ID: /taspr/area/19010208

CSV Export

To export the aggregated data in a CSV file, append ?format=csv.

Example: /temperature/area/19010208?format=csv

Example: /taspr/area/19010208?format=csv

8-digit HUC shapefiles accessed at https://datagateway.nrcs.usda.gov/
