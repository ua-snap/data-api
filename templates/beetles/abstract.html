Climate Protection from Spruce Beetles

The endpoints here provide access to modeled climate-related protections against spruce beetle population growth for future and historical scenarios. This data is for use in the Northern Climate Reports webapp, and have thus been clipped to the working domain of the Integrated Ecosystem Model (IEM) project.

Service endpoints

There are endpoints for point queries and aggregations (area summaries) for all available places.

  • Point Query: Climate protection from spruce beetles at a single point
  • Aggregate by AOI polygon ID: Climate protection from spruce beetles within an area of interest polygon, returning the mode of all data in polygon.

Dimensions:

  • era: Summary era:
    • 1988–2017
    • 2010–2039
    • 2040–2069
    • 2070–2099
  • model: AR5 model source, or Daymet:
    • Daymet
    • GFDL–ESM2M
    • HadGEM2–ES
    • MRI–CGCM3
    • NCAR–CCSM4
  • scenario: RCP scenario for AR5, or "Historical" for Daymet
    • Historical
    • rcp45
    • rcp85
  • snowpack: Relative snowpack used to model climate protection against spruce beetle spread
    • low
    • medium
Variables::
  • climate-protection: (text), high, minimal, or none
  • percent-high-protection: (float), percentage of high climate protection pixels in polygon AOI
  • percent-minimal-protection: (float), percentage of minimal climate protection pixels in polygon AOI
  • percent-no-protection: (float), percentage of no climate protection pixels in polygon AOI
